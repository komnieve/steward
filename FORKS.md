# Forks

Practice Layers people have authored and shared. Fork freely, share if it helps
others — gift economy, no gatekeeping. To add yours, PR a row here linking to your
repo or to a subdirectory under
[`practice-layer/examples/`](practice-layer/examples/).

| Layer | Who it's for |
|-------|--------------|
| [`contemplative-practitioner`](practice-layer/examples/contemplative-practitioner/) | Someone with an established contemplative practice — Buddhist/meditation-tradition vocabulary, work and practice held as one domain. Ships in this repo. |
