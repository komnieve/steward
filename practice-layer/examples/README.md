# Example Practice Layers

*Fully-written Practice Layers authored by users or communities that have adapted
Steward for their context. Fork whichever fits best, edit to fit you, copy into
`~/.steward/practice/`.*

## What's shipped

- [`contemplative-practitioner/`](contemplative-practitioner/) — for someone with an
  established contemplative practice. Draws on Buddhist/meditation-tradition
  vocabulary (Pāli fetters, Ajahn Chah, Ramana Maharshi, etc.). Opinionated and
  deep. Good starting point for meditators; excellent reference for non-meditators
  who want to see what depth looks like.

## Wanted

PRs welcome for any of these. They'd fill gaps the `contemplative-practitioner`
example can't:

- A **Stoic** layer drawing on Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, Epictetus, Musonius Rufus
- A **Christian contemplative** layer drawing on the Examen, Lectio Divina, desert
  fathers, Thomas Merton
- A **secular humanist** layer grounded in values clarification and ACT
- A **service-oriented** layer for volunteers and community organizers
- A **creator-oriented** layer for writers, artists, craftspeople
- A **parenting-while-working** layer

Open a PR adding a subdirectory with your version. Include a short `README.md`
explaining who it's for and what tradition or framing it draws from. Specificity
is the point. Generic wellness prose is exactly what we're avoiding.

Gift economy: fork freely, share when it serves, no gatekeeping.
